Hi! I'm Joseph

I am a second year student at the University of Michigan, and I'm interested in accessible web development and data science! When I started college, I was planning on majoring in computer science, but after taking both EECS and SI classes, I realized I wanted to study something more people-oriented.

I see the School of Information's interdisciplinary focus on technology and people as a perfect fit for my passions and goals. I am most interested in studying accessibility in user experience, to pursue a career in user experience design. I am fascinated by the ways in which information technology can more than impersonal problem solving, and how it can be human-oriented and exciting. My shift from CS to Information was caused by a love for people and problem solving in a people-oriented way.

I currently work at University of Michigan ITS as a Tech Consultant. I provide technical support for customers’ personal devices and Campus Computing Site technology, which allows me to combine my technical expertise with my desire to work with and help people.
